We Are Grateful for This Community

Fighting referee abuse is not a solo effort. We are deeply thankful to every individual, organization, and voice out there sharing the same vision — a game where officials are treated with the dignity and respect they deserve. The community we've found along the way makes all the difference.

Organizations

🛡️

Officially Human

US-Based · Multi-Sport

A leading US-based organization focused on restoring respect to officials across multiple sports. Officially Human works to humanize the officials through advocacy, education, and community building.

🌐 officiallyhuman.com
📣

RefShout

UK-Based · Anti-Abuse Campaign

A UK-based anti-abuse campaign dedicated to standing up for referees and fighting verbal and physical abuse at all levels of the game. RefShout amplifies the stories that need to be heard.

𝕏 @Ref_Shout
